## Idea Prioritization (Impact vs Feasibility)

| Idea | Impact | Feasibility | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| Credit Card Approval Prediction | High | High | ✅ Selected |
| Loan Default Risk Prediction | High | Medium | 2 |
| Fraud Detection | High | Low (data availability) | 3 |
| Customer Churn Prediction | Medium | High | 4 |

## Final Selected Idea
**Credit Card Approval Prediction** — chosen because it directly addresses a real, high-frequency problem faced by banks: manual, slow, and inconsistent credit card approval decisions. The team had access to a suitable dataset, relevant ML skills (XGBoost, Decision Trees, Scikit-Learn), and could realistically build and deploy a working solution within the project timeline.
