# Empathy Map — Bank Credit Officer (End User)

## SAYS
- "I need to review applications faster without compromising accuracy."
- "Too many good applicants get rejected because of inconsistent manual checks."

## THINKS
- "Is there a way to reduce bias and human error in approval decisions?"
- "I wish I had a quick, reliable way to assess applicant risk."

## DOES
- Manually cross-checks income, employment, and credit history documents
- Escalates unclear cases to senior officers, causing delays
- Uses spreadsheets and static rules to shortlist applications

## FEELS
- Overwhelmed by application volume during peak periods
- Frustrated by inconsistent outcomes across different reviewers
- Anxious about approving high-risk applicants by mistake

## PAINS
- Slow turnaround time for applicants
- Risk of human bias affecting decisions
- No standardized, data-backed scoring system

## GAINS (Expected from the solution)
- Faster, automated approval predictions
- Consistent, data-driven decision-making
- Reduced unnecessary rejections and default risk
