# Define Problem Statement

## Problem Statement
Banks and financial institutions receive a large volume of credit card applications every day. Reviewing each application manually — checking income, employment history, credit history, and demographic details — is time-consuming, inconsistent across reviewers, and prone to human bias. This leads to delays in decision-making, unnecessary rejections of eligible applicants, and approvals that carry higher-than-expected risk.

## Problem Statement Template (Who–What–Why)
- **Who** faces the problem: Banks, credit card issuers, and loan officers
- **What** is the problem: Manual, slow, and inconsistent credit card approval decisions
- **Why** it matters: Leads to revenue loss, poor customer experience, and inconsistent risk assessment

## Refined Problem Statement
"How might we automate the credit card approval process using historical applicant data so that banks can make faster, consistent, and data-driven approval decisions while reducing unnecessary application rejections and default risk?"
