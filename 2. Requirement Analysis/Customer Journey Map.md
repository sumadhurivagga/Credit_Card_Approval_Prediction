# Customer Journey Map — Credit Card Applicant

| Stage | Applicant Action | Touchpoint | Emotion | Opportunity |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Awareness | Learns about credit card offer | Bank website/app | Curious | Clear eligibility info upfront |
| Application | Fills application form with personal & financial details | Web form / Streamlit app | Hopeful | Simple, guided input fields |
| Processing | Waits for approval decision | Backend ML model | Anxious | Instant prediction, no long wait |
| Decision | Receives Approved / Rejected result | Result screen | Relief or disappointment | Clear, honest explanation of outcome |
| Post-Decision | Uses card (if approved) or seeks alternatives (if rejected) | Email/SMS notification | Satisfied or frustrated | Transparent, unbiased reasoning |

## Key Insight
The biggest opportunity to improve applicant experience is at the **Processing** and **Decision** stages — replacing slow manual review with an instant, consistent ML-based prediction significantly reduces applicant anxiety and wait time.
